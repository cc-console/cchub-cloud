🔒 你的数据,始终在你自己电脑上Your data stays on your own machine

你和 AI 的对话、代码、文件,全部在你本机完成和保存。cc-console 本身只是一个传输工具 —— 把你本地终端的画面安全地转发到你的手机/浏览器,不存储、不分析你的任何会话内容。Your conversations with the AI, your code, your files — all happen and stay on your machine. cc-console is just a transport: it securely relays your local terminal to your phone/browser, and never stores or inspects any of your session content.

把本机端口直接免费映射到公网(如临时隧道)既不稳定也不安全。所以我们提供私有、稳定、加密的专属隧道,绑定到你自己的 你的名字.cchub.cloud 地址,带 token 鉴权 —— 公测期间免费。Exposing a local port to the public internet for free (e.g. throwaway tunnels) is neither stable nor secure. So we run a private, stable, encrypted tunnel bound to your own your-name.cchub.cloud address with token auth — free during the public beta.

cc-console 把你桌面上的 AI 编程会话(Claude Code / Codex / Gemini)实时搬到手机、平板和任意浏览器:远程实时观看、随手接管、断线自动重连。不仅是「远程托管」—— 它还会在 agent 需要你拍板 Yes/No 时主动「滴滴」叫你,或交给 Autopilot 自动确认。一次部署,随处掌控。Your AI coding agents run on your desktop, but you can't sit there all day. cc-console brings their live sessions (Claude Code / Codex / Gemini) to your phone, tablet, or any browser: watch in real time, take over with a tap, and never drop the connection. It's more than remote hosting — it chirps for you the moment an agent needs a Yes/No, or hands that off to Autopilot. Deploy once, control from anywhere.
